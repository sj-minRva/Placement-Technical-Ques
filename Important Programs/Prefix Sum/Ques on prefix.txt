‚≠ê 5. Bonus: 2D Prefix Sum Formula (for matrix problems)

If you ever need submatrix sum:

prefix[i][j] = matrix[i][j] 
             + prefix[i-1][j] 
             + prefix[i][j-1] 
             - prefix[i-1][j-1]


Allows O(1) submatrix range queries.
